history

Hyderabad, founded in 1591 by Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah, has a rich heritage that blends various cultures and influences. Originally a center for the diamond and pearl trade, it became the capital of the Nizams, who were known for their opulence and patronage of arts and architecture.

Geography

Located in the Deccan Plateau, Hyderabad is characterized by its rocky terrain and artificial lakes. The city has a tropical climate, with the best times to visit being from October to March when temperatures are mild.

Demographics

"Hyderabad is one of the most diverse cities in India, with a population of over 10 million. The primary languages spoken are Telugu and Urdu, along with English, reflecting its multicultural fabric."
